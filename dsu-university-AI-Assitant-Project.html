About Us

Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University (DSU) is established under the Tamil Nadu Private Universities Act, 2019, located in Perambalur, Tamil Nadu, India. DSU offers multidisciplinary academic programs including medicine and engineering, guided by its motto "Education for the Real World".

Admission Procedure

  • 1
    Make an application

    Choose a programme / course
  • 3
    Check eligibility and requirements
  • 4
    Take online entrance exam DSU-JET (Engineering only)
  • 5
    Attend counselling / interview (for some courses)
  • 6
    Receive confirmation or rejection
  • 7
    Pay fees if selected
  • 8
    Start the programme 🎉
